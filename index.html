About

Established in 2013 Power Chord is Oregon State University's mixed acapella club. The member of Power Chord represent a variety of majors, but a common love of making music brings us together. Come see us sing every Friday on the MU steps with Outspoken and Divine! 5 PM on Friday!

McKenna

McKenna is a junior studying Marketing and this is her third year singing alto in Power Chord. This year, she is also serving as the group’s president! McKenna loves PC because it gives her an opportunity to forget about the stress of school or anything else for a couple of hours and just make music with her friends. Outside of the group, McKenna, like Bri, is an active member of Kappa Alpha Theta and she has three pet cats (one of which her parents replaced her with when she left for college). Lastly, McKenna and Tasnia were roommates during their freshman year, which is a running joke that the two of them never let the rest of the group forget.

  • Position: President
  • Voice: Alto
  • Major: Marketing

Natalie

Natalie is a senior studying Food Science and Technology with a focus in Fermentation (she brews her own hard cider at home!). She also has minors in Chemistry and German, and even got to study abroad for a few months in Germany! This is her fourth and final year in the group singing as both an alto and a tenor, depending on the song. Natalie’s favorite part of the group is the fact that it is always changing, so she gets to meet and know so many new people. Natalie has seen the group grow and served as President, Treasurer and Secretary in the past, and Secretary again this year. Natalie is a sweetheart with a rich, smooth voice that always takes our breath away. We will definitely miss her next year, but she is from Corvallis so we know we’ll see her again soon!
